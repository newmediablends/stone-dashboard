### Jay Shetty — Communicate Effectively (watched today)

- Regulate before you speak — hold back emotion, then respond.
- Clarity beats intensity — fewer words, not more. "When this happens I feel overlooked."
- People don't argue with facts, they argue with threats — lower threat first. "Here's what I'm seeing — tell me if I'm missing something."
- Questions change everything — "Help me understand..."
- Tone carries more than words — conversation vs. conflict.
- Close the loop — "Here's what we agreed on. Here's what happens next."
